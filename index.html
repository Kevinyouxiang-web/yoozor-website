About the Group

A Legacy of Precision Manufacturing

Yoozor stands as the dedicated export arm of Ningbo Fuda Intelligent Technology, a manufacturing powerhouse with over two decades of expertise in climate control.

Operating from a 50,000m² smart facility in Ningbo, Zhejiang Province, our production line integrates automated assembly, rigorous quality testing, and ISO-certified processes. Every unit undergoes comprehensive performance validation before shipment.

Our R&D team of 20+ engineers continuously advances energy efficiency, noise reduction, and smart connectivity — ensuring our partners receive market-leading products.
