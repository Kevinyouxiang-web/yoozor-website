About Yoozor

Manufacturing excellence, global reach — we are the dedicated export partner of one of China's leading climate control technology companies.

Our Story

Building the Future of Climate Control

Founded in 1998, Ningbo Fuda Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. has grown from a specialized electronics manufacturer into a leading OEM/ODM platform for portable air conditioners and dehumidifiers.

Ningbo Yoozor Trading Co., Ltd. serves as the authorized export division, connecting global brands and distributors with Fuda's manufacturing capabilities. Together, we deliver over 500,000 units annually to partners in 30+ countries.

Our 50,000m² smart facility in Ningbo, Zhejiang Province, operates with ISO 9001-certified processes, automated assembly lines, and comprehensive testing laboratories — ensuring every product meets rigorous international quality standards.
