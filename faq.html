Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, OEM/ODM process, certifications, and shipping.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for OEM orders?

Our standard MOQ for OEM orders is 500–1,000 units per model, depending on the product complexity and customization level. For ODM projects or custom development, MOQ discussions are handled on a case-by-case basis.

What certifications can you provide for your products?

We support a comprehensive range of certifications including CE, CB, ETL, GS, RoHS, REACH, and Energy Star. Our engineering team manages the certification process for your target market — whether EU, North America, or Asia.

What is the typical lead time for an OEM order?

Standard lead time is 30–45 days after sample approval and deposit. Custom ODM projects typically require 60–90 days for tooling, prototyping, and testing. We provide a detailed production schedule for every order.

Can you customize the product design and branding?

Absolutely. Our OEM/ODM services include full customization: product design, PCB layout, color scheme, branding (logo, packaging, manuals), voltage/plug configuration, and user interface design. Your brand, our manufacturing expertise.

What shipping terms do you offer?

We offer flexible shipping terms including FOB (Ningbo), CIF, and DDP depending on your preference. Our location in Ningbo — one of the world's busiest ports — ensures competitive freight rates and reliable shipping schedules.

How do you ensure product quality?

Quality is embedded in every step: IQC (incoming quality control) for all raw materials, IPQC (in-process quality checks) during assembly, and 100% functional testing before packaging. Our facility is ISO 9001 certified, and third-party inspections are welcome.

Can you help with product certification for my country?

Yes. Our compliance team has extensive experience with global certification requirements. We can manage the entire certification process for your target market, including documentation, sample testing, and liaison with certification bodies.

What after-sales support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive after-sales support including warranty service, technical troubleshooting, spare parts supply, and continuous product improvement. Our dedicated support team is available across multiple communication channels.
