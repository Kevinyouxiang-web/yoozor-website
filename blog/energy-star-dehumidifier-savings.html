Beyond Dollars: What Energy Star Actually Tests

  • ✅ Integrated Energy Factor (IEF) — Liters of water removed per kWh. Energy Star requires ≥1.85 L/kWh for units 25-50 pints. Our FDD16-521X achieves 2.2 L/kWh.
  • ✅ Standby Power — Must use ≤2 watts when idle. Many non-certified units draw 5-10W in standby.
  • ✅ DOE 2024 Test Standard — New test conditions at 65°F/60% RH are more realistic than the old 80°F standard. All Yoozor units are 2024-compliant.
